NLM21

Nonlinear Meeting on Zoom
March 22-23, 2021

The NLM21 is the 6th of a series of annual workshops focusing on nonlinear analysis and differential problems. This edition's common thread will cover theoretical topics on reaction-diffusion equations and dynamical systems with possible applications to biology or physics.
